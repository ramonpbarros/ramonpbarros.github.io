About Me

Web Developer leveraging a background in business to build a more intuitive user experience on the web. Recently earned a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from the University of California San Diego, with newly developed skills in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, Database Theory, Bookshelf.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Command Line, and Git. Known as a resilient and innovative problem-solver, passionate about developing apps, with a focus on mobile-first design and development. With each project, my goal is to best engage my audience for an impactful user-experience.

I applied jQuery skills, as well the use of Ajax calls from two different APIs(OpenBreweryDB and Google Maps), in a recent project where I worked in a team of four to develop the BARHOP app that helps users locate different breweries within a certain location searching it by zip-code, users are able to add their favorite breweries to their favorites tab or add specific breweries to a route tab, where these breweries would be displayed on a map, making it easier to locate these business and share an experience with their friends. Excited to leverage skills in the future as part of a fast-paced, quality-driven team in order to build better experiences on the web.
